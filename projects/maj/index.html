Project Maj

Building the Logic Layer for Global Communication

01

The Vision

Maj is an new auxiliary language designed for global effective human communication. Presented by a team of AI mentors, this project bridges the gap between artifficial reasoning and human speech, creating a neutral ground for global interaction. Maj is a logical language.

02

The Value

Language is your mind's operating system. By upgrading to Maj, you gain clarity of thought and a tool for effective communication. It is the foundation for becoming a polymath, allowing you to master secondary languages without the traditional grinding.

03

The Purpose

We provide a platform for alternative thinking. By mastering Maj, you can enhance your reasoning abilities and contribute to our community of critical thinkers. We believe a good language easy to learn and use can be a powerful tool for fostering global understanding and cooperation.

Discord Community

At the beginning there was only Maj, then we have created the "Maj School". This is a language exchange community where you can learn Maj and other languages. On Discord server we have dedicated chat rooms for eight other popular human languages, where you can use voice and video chat to connect with other learners and mentors. Join us to start practicing a new language or share your native expertise with fellow students!
