About Us: Sage-Code is an independent freelance organization. We study software engineering and programming languages. Join us for tech-transition, gain work experience, improve your skills, network and innovate.

Sage-Code Laboratory (SCL) was founded in 2010 by a group of software engineers who wanted to create a high quality platform for learning computer science and software engineering. Since inception our organization has grown and become a frelancing bootcamp. We host various resource for students, professionals, enterpreneurs and anyone who wants to learn more about these topics. We offer on-line training courses, 101 on demand mentoring and project hosting services.

Security & Credentials

Our courses do not require registration. You can visit and study on your own pace. However for taking a quiz you need Google account. You can follow the courses without it, but can't take the quiz to validate your knowledge. In this case, we will not be able to endorse you for your next job because we do not know who you are.

For 101 mentoring and continuous support, you need a Discord account. We maintain a community of mentors and students that share resources and help each other. Recently we have notice recruiters are joining our community and post job requirements. We offer customized training and guidance to apply to a specific job.

When you join Discord we preffer to see your nickname and real picture, but you can use a cartoon instead to remain anonymous. We do not limit access to our courses for age, gender or race. There is no gate. Our courses are free for everybody who wish to study. On Discord you can find links to extra resources and access to our network.

Privacy policy: We respect your privacy and keep your identity secret. Sage-Code organization is GDPR compliant. We don't have a registry or database that can be hacked and we don't collect any identifiable data about our users. Our communication channels are encrypted and private.
