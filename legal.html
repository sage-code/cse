Legal Notice

Transparency, Privacy, and Independence.

01. Privacy

Sage-Code is GDPR compliant by design. We do not maintain a central database of personal identities. Your progress is stored locally in your browser. We do not track you, nor collect or sell data to third parties. If you register to become VIP member, you can claim the right to be forgotten and opt-out any time.

02. Affiliation

Sage-Code Laboratory (SCL) is an independent community. We are not affiliated with Sage Group plc accounting nor Sage Math nor Sage programming language. We use to host sagecode.pro and sagecode.net but cost was too high. So we register sagecode.org on April 17 2026 for next 5 years.

03. Mentorship

We offer free mentoring trials (42 hours /member) on Discord in private video calls. SCL reserves the right to manage community capacity to ensure quality of service. We accept 100 new students/year. Inactive members on Discord may be cycled out to maintain our 100-member cap. If you loose your membership you can't resume your free training. You have to subscribe as VIP member to continue.

04. Ownership

All original laboratory resources and manuals are the property of SCL. However, we believe in technical sovereignty: any code you develop based on our teachings belongs to you. No artificial gates, no hidden royalties. You can use our project list to register a project with us. We list your project but we do not make special promotions and you can de-list your project at any time.
