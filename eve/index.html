What is EVE?

Eve is going to be a declarative 4'th generation language. Eve is descendent from another language we have design called Level in 2002. We have scrap Level project and the compiler we have created for Level. Eve is more modern, feature reach and comprehensible.

Choosing Eve name is from a movie that I liked called Wall-E. Eve was this little robot that could fly arround, who fall in love with an older robot called Wall-E. They have adventures together in space and they have an important role to play in the future of human kind. There are many videos on YouTube about these two cute robots. Eve is very fast and asvanced but also has a strong purpose and a sensitive sould.

EVE Purpose

Eve is a general purpose languages that works in EVE environment. It's primary purpose is to transfer data from one database into external files, and load data from external files back into the same or another database. Also Eve should be able to compress data, synchronize two databases by using a bi-directional connection, curate, encrypt, archive and restore large databases as efficient as possible. EVE features can be extended using Eve language.
