The Manifesto

Engineering principles for the sovereign developer.

01. Open Intelligence

We reject artificial gates designed for self-interest. Knowledge should not be hidden behind paywalls or unnecessary credentials. We believe everyone can learn when provided with the right resources and a clear path.

02. Human Initiative

We believe in the limitless human capacity to invent, create, and innovate. Motivation and individual initiative are the primary drivers of technical progress. Our role is to provide the spark; yours is to fuel the fire.

03. AI Verification

We appreciate AI as a powerful collaborator, but we do not outsource our responsibility. We keep a strict tab on every line of code generated. If you didn't verify it, you didn't write it.

04. Radical Ownership

The sovereign developer understands their tools. We move away from blind consumption of frameworks and move toward architectural mastery. To innovate, you must first understand the foundation you stand upon.
