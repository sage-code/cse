Supercomputer Centers

A supercomputer center is actually a network of powerful computers used for scientific applications. Many non profit organizations have access to these networks and can run applications remotely on several machines.

  • NRSC = National Research Supercomputer Center
  • SDSC = San Diego Supercomputer Center
  • OSC = Ohio Supercomputer Center
