Polyglot School

This is a virtual school dedicated to teaching you a secondary language. We believe that Language learning skills are essential for personal and professional grouth. Our school is open for everyone who wants to develop his personal skills and learn a new language. We offer content for 8 languages, including our own Maj language.

Learning Method

We offer digital books, presentations and videos. Our books contain QR codes that link to videos and quizzes. When you read a book, you can use phone or tablet to scan QR code and you can watch the videos on YouTube. You need the book to read along then study grammar and expressions. This method do not require any help from a professor or a mentor.

Our method is explained in this first book: "Discover Maj, Artifficial Language". This book is free to download for anyone who visit our Ko-Fi shop. You can set price 0 or pay what you want. For this book you do not need a subscription and you can download the book many times to get a fresh copy.
