What is Maj School?

We have created Maj School in 2012, two years after Sage-Code. Our purpose was to create a new language called Maj, for games and chat. This language is not yet finished. We are looking for volunteers. "Maj School" offer language exchange and free resources. In return, we hope to recrute volunteers that can learn and improve Maj.
