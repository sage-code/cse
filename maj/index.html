About Maj School

Welcome to Maj School! This is a virtual school dedicated to teaching you a secondary language. We believe that Language learning skills are essential for personal and professional grouth. Our school is open for everyone who wants to develop his personal skills and learn a new language. Maj School goes beyound learning a new language, we also create our own language.

Our Learning Method

Maj school offer digital books (PDF) and printed books, on Amazon. These books contain QR code that link to videos and quizzes. You can watch the videos on YouTube for free, but the videos are not self sufficient. You need the book to read along, study grammar and expressions. We do not have yet an app except for Discord, where you can talk to our members.

Polyglot Resources

We use Google docs to create "Maj Lexicon". This is a Polyglot dictionary of 8 languages. This document can be used to learn basic words for one of 8 languages we study. In this dictionary words are organized by length in different tabs. Short words first tab (W2), longer words next tabs (W3,W4,W5,W6). This dictionary is a primitive data resource for making an APP.
