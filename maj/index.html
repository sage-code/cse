Maj School

Enhance Your Human Operating System. Learn a secondary language without a professor.

We believe that language is the core of your personal operating system. At Maj School, we provide the tools to not just learn a new language, but to master the art of communication itself. Our mission is to help you become a true polyglot and unlock your full potential.

Interactive Learning

Learning is about connection, not just memorization! Our virtual polyglot school is built on a vibrant community where you can actively practice your skills. Engage with other members, join our Discord, and participate in quizzes to make learning both effective and fun. We host public events and one-on-one sessions to give you plenty of opportunities to build fluency and make new friends.

Maj School (Discord)

Maj School is a language exchange community on Discord with dedicated chat rooms for each language. You can use voice and video to communicate directly with other learners. Join us and start learning your favorite new language or share your native one!
