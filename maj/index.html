The Maj Alphabet Foundation

At the heart of our language learning journey is the Maj alphabet. We provide detailed explanations using the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) to ensure you grasp the sounds accurately. With accompanying sound recordings, you'll master pronunciation in no time! You can easily learn the Maj alphabet here on this website. We have posted audio recordings.

Maj Dictionary

We use Google docs to create Maj dictionary. This include translation of Maj in 6 languages. This dictionary can be used to learn any language not only Maj. In this dictionary words are organized by length. Short words first tab, longer words next tabs. Remember, learn shortest words first then longer words. Shorter words are more frequent.
