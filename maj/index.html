About Maj School

Maj: is an artificial auxiliary language designed for International communication. Maj has two alphabets. Latin printed alphabet and Georgian inspired script. Maj School is the name of our Discord server where you can learn several other popular languages alongside Maj. We use AI to generate beautiful images and Google Translator to create a useful poliglot lexicon.
